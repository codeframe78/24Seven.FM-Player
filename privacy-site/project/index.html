Built for listening

More than a stream button

The Alpha brings the network's live listening and community workflows into an adaptive Android interface, while keeping station-specific behavior behind clear capability boundaries.

01

Playback that follows you

Service-owned Media3 playback, system media controls, audio focus, notification-to-app navigation, and automatic recovery after validated connectivity returns.

02

Live station context

Now-playing metadata and artwork, bounded Queue and History views, five-station switching, and station-scoped request availability.

03

Member workflows

Independent protected station sessions, chat, song requests, request history, membership details, and responsive 1,500-track Favorites lists.

04

Community safeguards

Adult and Terms gates, mature-content reveal, report-content and report-user flows, plus local station-scoped blocking and management.
